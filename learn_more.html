Receiving a diabetes diagnosis can be one of the stressful moments in your life. Our team's goal is to reduce your or a love one's stress as much as possible by minimizing the amount of work that you have to do in order to learn about this lifelong illness.

Important Topics

What are the types of diabetes?

Type 1 diabetes: Occurs when there is an insulin shortage in the body due to the immune system attacking insulin producing cells in the pancreas.
Type 2 diabetes: Occurs when the body cannot either produce enough insulin or use it properly.

Glossary

  • Insulin: A hormone that regulates blood sugar.
  • Glucose: A type of sugar in your blood.
  • HbA1c: A measure of your average blood glucose over 2-3 months.
